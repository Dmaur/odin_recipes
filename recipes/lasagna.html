Lasagna

Descripton

This traditional italian pasta cake is delicious and cheesy. Filling and hearty this dish is great for cool fall or winter days.

    Ingredients

  • Dried lasagna noodles
    • Ground beef or pork Tomato sauce
  • Spinach
  • Garlic
  • Yellow or spanish onion
  • Bechemel sauce
  • Mushrooms (optional)
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese

    Steps

  1. Boil noodles until soft. Shock cold and set aside
  2. Fine dice onion and garlic. Sautee on low/med heat until onions translucent
  3. Add ground meat to the pan and cook until fully browned
  4. Add tomato sauce to the pan and simmer on low for at least an hour
  5. Add oregano and basil to tomato sauce near the end of simmer time
  6. Starting with tomato sauce on the bottom of a casserole dish, layer sauce and noodles back and forth alternating tomato and bechemel. On tomato layers add ricotta. On bechemel layers add spinach
  7. Once you reach the top of your dish with noodles, add one more layer of tomato sauce then top with Mozzarella.
  8. Bake in oven @350 degrees coverd with foil for 45 min. after 45min remove foil and bake for another 15 min
  9. Once cheese on top is browned remove from oven and let set for 15 min
  10. Cut to desired size, serve and enjoy
