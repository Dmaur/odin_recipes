Saffron and cheese tart

Discription

This poor student classic is sure to feed you in a hurry and for cheap.

If your feeling rich try adding an egg or some frozen veg of your choice

    Ingredients

  • water, 1L
  • packet of ramen
  • small pot
  • bowl

    Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in small pot
  2. Open ramen pack, place noodles in boiling water. reserve flavor pack
  3. Boil noodles for roughly 2 min until "al dente"
  4. Strain noodles but save 1/4 of the water
  5. Transfer noodles and remaining water to a bowl and add flavor pack
  6. Stir in flavor pack and enjoy
